Care & Tips

Learn how to keep your Exquisite products in perfect condition!

Cake Care Tips

Storage

Store your cake in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For best freshness, keep it in an airtight container and refrigerate if not consuming within 24 hours.

Serving

Let the cake sit at room temperature for 30 minutes before serving to enhance its flavor. Use a sharp knife dipped in hot water for clean slices.

Freezing

Wrap individual slices in plastic wrap and place them in a freezer-safe bag. Freeze for up to 3 months. Thaw in the fridge overnight before enjoying.

Lip Gloss Care Tips

Storage

Keep your lip gloss in a cool, dry place away from heat sources. Avoid leaving it in direct sunlight or hot environments to prevent melting.

Application

Apply on clean, exfoliated lips for a smooth finish. Use a lip liner for precision, and avoid over-applying to prevent stickiness.

Cleaning

Wipe the applicator with a clean tissue after each use to prevent bacteria buildup. If the gloss thickens, gently warm the tube in your hands to restore its texture.

Face Sheet Care Tips

Storage

Store face sheets in a cool, dry place. Keep them sealed in their packaging to maintain moisture and prevent drying out.

Application

Apply on a clean face after cleansing. Leave the sheet on for 15-20 minutes, then remove and massage the remaining essence into your skin.

Aftercare

Follow up with a moisturizer to lock in hydration. Avoid rinsing your face after use to maximize the benefits of the essence.
